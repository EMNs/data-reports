How AI-Powered Deal Intelligence Works for GTM
Version 1.0 · Business Stakeholders · March 2026

Goal

This document outlines the architecture for delivering next best action recommendations to Account Executives — combining CRM, call intelligence, product usage, and sales engagement data into a single AI-powered system that tells AEs what to do, why, and when.

The Deal CoPilot is the first implementation: a daily Slack briefing with the top 3 prioritized actions per AE, powered by data products as foundational building blocks which provide basic context, Snowflake Cortex as the intelligence layer, and delivered via Workato.

Architecture at a Glance

The system has three layers, each with a clear purpose:

Evaluation Layer
Continuous quality measurement of agent outputs
Business Evals Automated Scoring Feedback Loops
Agent Intelligence · Snowflake Cortex
Reasoning layer — applies rules, skills, and memory to generate recommendations
Rules Skills Memory Corrections
Data Context Foundation
Data product composed of 4 sub-data-products
Conversations Organization Product Usage Engagement

How It All Connects

Source systems feed directly into data products in Snowflake — all data ingestion and transformation happens inside the data platform. Workato sits on the output side, delivering AI-generated recommendations to the right people and systems.

The Four Data Products

Each data product answers a specific business question about every deal:

💬 Conversations

"What happened on the last call?"
Champion hasn't been on a call in 3 weeks — MEDDPICC gap on Economic Buyer

🏢 Organization

"Who is this account and where is the deal?"
Enterprise account, $180K ARR, Stage 4, closing in 22 days

📈 Product Usage

"Are they actually using the product?"
MAU dropped 30% this month — churn risk score is HIGH

🤝 Engagement

"Are we staying in touch?"
No outreach in 16 days — last email had 0% reply rate
Together, they form a 360-degree deal view that no single tool provides on its own.

Snowflake Cortex — The AI Engine

Snowflake Cortex is the built-in AI layer that runs directly inside Snowflake, where the data already lives. Intelligence is applied without moving data out — keeping it secure, governed, and fast.

🔍 Cortex Analyst

AEs or managers ask questions in plain English: "Which deals are at risk of slipping this quarter?"
Self-service insights without waiting for a report

🧭 Cortex Search

Finds relevant deal context across all four data products using semantic understanding
Agent retrieves exactly the right context for each recommendation

📝 Summarize

Condenses conversation transcripts and engagement history into digestible briefs
3-sentence deal summary instead of reading 5 call transcripts

😊 Sentiment Analysis

Scores stakeholder sentiment from call data and email tone
Early warning when a champion is disengaging

🏷️ Classification

Categorizes deals by risk level, expansion potential, engagement health
Automated deal scoring without manual tagging

🧠 Complete (LLM)

Generates AE recommendations, email drafts, and coaching suggestions
The "brain" that turns data into actionable advice

Why Cortex (vs. External AI)?

  • Data stays in Snowflake — no copies, no external API calls with sensitive deal data
  • Governed by the same access policies — PII and sensitivity tags are enforced
  • Scales with the data platform — no separate infrastructure to manage
  • Cost-efficient — compute runs on Snowflake credits, no separate LLM billing

Workato — Action & Delivery
